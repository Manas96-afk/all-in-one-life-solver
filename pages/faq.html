Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about Life Problem Solver.

Are the AI tools free to use?

The platform includes a demo mode that works out-of-the-box. If you want more powerful or longer responses, you can configure your own free or paid API keys in the Settings.

Does Life Solver store my conversations?

Your recent conversations are only stored locally on your device in your browser's local storage so you can look at them later. We do not store them on any centralized databases.

How do I add my own OpenAI/Gemini API key?

Navigate to any tool like the "Life Advisor", click the "Demo Mode" or gear icon button in the header, and paste your API key in the configuration settings. Your key is stored locally on your computer.

Is my information private?

Yes, because we do not have a centralized database, all of your tool history and favorites are completely private to your device.
