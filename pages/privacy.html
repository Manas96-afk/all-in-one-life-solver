Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2024

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the information necessary to provide you with our AI-powered life-solving tools. Currently, all data generated by your use of the tools (like your problem history and tool preferences) is stored locally on your device using your browser's Local Storage.

2. Use of Your Information

  • To personalize your experience in the Dashboard.
  • To track recent queries so you can retrieve past solutions easily in the tool template.
  • We do NOT sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties.

3. Use of AI APIs

When you connect an API Key (like OpenAI or Gemini) to use the advanced features of Life Solver, your queries are sent directly from your browser to the respective API provider. We do not intercept, store, or monitor these requests on any centralized server.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@lifesolver.com.
